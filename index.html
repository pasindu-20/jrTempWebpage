What is hackX Jr. 8.0

hackX Jr. 8.0, which is set for 2025, hosted by the Industrial Management Science Students’ Association of the Department of Industrial Management, University of Kelaniya, is a landmark event turning school students' innovative ideas into reality.

Under the theme "Give Shapes to Ideas", hackX Jr. is more than just a competition; it’s a transformative journey encouraging participants to go beyond theory and find tech-based solutions for real-world problems.
